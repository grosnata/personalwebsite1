My Trip Overview

Summer in Sweden

During the summer of 2019, I was fortunate enough to spend 8 weeks in Stockholm, Sweden. I visited this beautiful country through an internship program - Sage Corps. I spent the summer working at Sensivo, a technology startup. Most importantly, I was able to gain 7 new friends! As a group of 8 total undergraduates, we were from all over the United States - streching from coast to coast!
